Behind SkyAware

I am a student software developer and an aspiring pilot currently preparing for the FAA Private Pilot License (PPL). SkyAware is the result of my passion for bridging the gap between aviation safety and modern software engineering.

By combining AI with real-world aviation data, I aim to create tools that make the skies safer for every pilot, from student to professional.
