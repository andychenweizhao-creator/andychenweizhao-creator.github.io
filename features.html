Dynamic Inflight Support

Once airborne, SkyAware transforms into a proactive safety companion. Emergency Response Mode

When seconds matter, SkyAware's Emergency Mode provides instant, high-contrast guidance to the nearest suitable landing site. The AI automatically parses engine data and glide performance to calculate an optimal recovery path, while simultaneously preparing emergency frequency presets and airport diagrams. This focused interface strips away non-essential data, ensuring that the pilot remains calm and directed during critical system failures.
